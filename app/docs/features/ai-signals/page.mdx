# AI Signals

AI signals are designed to be informative, inspectable, and easy to question.

<h2 id="how-signals-work">How Signals Work</h2>

The AI layer produces advisory recommendations such as `buy`, `sell`, or `hold` based on the
inputs and strategies defined in the current system. What matters most for the user is that the
signal is presented as guidance, not as a command.

<h2 id="confidence-and-reasoning">Confidence and Reasoning</h2>

Every serious advisory flow should answer two questions:

1. What is the system suggesting?
2. Why is it suggesting it?

CryptoDesk AI answers both by exposing confidence and reasoning rather than pretending that a
single label is enough.

<h2 id="risk-filtering">Risk Filtering</h2>

Risk filtering is intentionally visible. If the system decides an advisory is too risky, it can
downgrade that recommendation to `hold`. This protects the user and teaches them how the model is
being constrained.

<Callout tone="info" title="Transparency over hype">
The product avoids black-box AI language on purpose. The goal is trust through clarity, not
confidence theater.
</Callout>
